How to play

  • Pick a difficulty and a symbol theme, then press Start.
  • All cards begin face down.
  • Click two cards on each turn to flip them.
  • If the symbols match, the pair stays face up. If not, they flip back.
  • Clear the whole board by finding every pair.
  • Win in as few moves and as little time as you can. Your star rating reflects how efficiently you played.
